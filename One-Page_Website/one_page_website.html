Welcome to the fansite for FFVII Remake!

Thanks for visiting, we will have all kinds of information on the FFVII Remake once the site is complete.
Please pardon the dust as the website is fleshed out.

FFVII Remake is a reimagining of the Playstation 1 classic of the same name.
It is scheduled to be released on April 10th, 2020, although many people have stated
they have received a physical copy almost a week earlier.

The battle system for the new game will be a combination of the old Active-Time Battle System and the newer free moving style of Final Fantasy XV.

The developers have spent almost five years planning and building the remake from the ground up. It has been one of the most widely anticipated remakes in gaming history.
